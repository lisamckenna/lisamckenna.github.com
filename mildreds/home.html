Welcome to Mildred's Corner Cafe!

Some of our customers say walking into Mildred's is like being in someone's home. If you like a home that's quaint, colorful and very friendly then you've found the right place.

We pride ourselves in friendly service, a fun atmosphere and great food. We have all your standard breakfast offerings...and lots of unexpected ones, like our specials that change with the seasons. We also carry Loose-leaf teas in 25 flavors served in their own individual teapots.

While you're waiting, take a look around Old Soul vintage gift shop and see if there isn't something that might tempt you for that special place in your home.

We promise we are not like most breakfast joints, and because we're small, you may very well sit next to someone and find you make a new friend before breakfast is over. So come on by!
