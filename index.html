Hello World!

My name is Lisa McKenna, it's a wonder I have a name since I was nameless for weeks after I was born. Mom wanted Jennifer. Dad didn't, as a jenny is a donkey in Ireland and his girl wouldn't be named after a donkey (or worse). He wanted his girl named Lisa Marie, like Elvis. And he obviously won.

My Journey so Far.

I had plans to be a painting major at MassArt, but switched it around when I fell in love with typography. I hand cut letterforms 'til my fingertips bled, and manipulated fonts like crazy. What I loved about MassArt was its focus on the business of design, which is rare. It helped lessen the blow when, perusing a cosmetics counter at Macy's, I discovered a comp I designed as a lowly-intern was mass produced for an entire Estee Lauder fragrance line. Best lesson for a green designer. Undeterred, I co-founded a graphic design studio where I've chartered tons of innovative projects with awesome clients for 20 years. In 2010 I founded a second company which will be focused on UX design and development for iOS, web and e-Bookery. I'm really excited about the RampUp, its community and being able to fuse my design skills with understanding code. I'm tired of turning down web jobs. I love that I've discovered how intuitive html is and I look forward to using a font other than Times Roman soon.

What Else?

I have two hilarious kids. I'm a first gen American obtaining dual citizenship. I love the sound of bowling alleys. My husband will vouch I cook a mean ribeye. I have an affinity for Scandinavian design. I'm inspired daily by the Holstee Manifesto. I've owned way too many Mac products in my life, but have always wished for an Apple Lisa, probably not if my name was Jenny. Thanks for reading!
